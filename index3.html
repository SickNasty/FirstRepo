How the Kings Compare too Other Teams

The kings are in the western conference which means they play all teams in the west. They have to go up against team such as the sharks and ducks. But within a conference there are divisions. The kings are in the Pacific Division while there is the Central Division with the Blackhawks, Avalanche etc. The Kings only play the teams in their own division. But when playoffs come around and you have to play for the conference championship the two divisions will end up playing eachother. But back to what I was saying about how they compare to the other teams. They are one of the best in the Pacific Division in my opinion but they definetly have some good compitition. They have to play the sharks who made it to the finals this year. They also have to play against the Oilers who have a very special player. They have Connor Mcdavid who went first overall in the NHl Draft and is definetly a star player that any hockey fan knows about.

Overall the Kings havent been a great team in their 47 year history but they have definetly become a team that is not a joke. I think ever since Luc Robitaille and Wayne Gretzky the team has been getting better. It did take them a good while to win the Stanley cup but to do it in 2012 and 2014 is impressive. Their all-star line up made then winners of what I think is the most coveted trophy in sports.
